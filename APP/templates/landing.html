Our Story

The Lantern Readers Club is a space where stories come alive through conversation. Whether you’re a casual reader or a devoted book lover, our club creates a welcoming environment to share perspectives, discover new genres, and build meaningful connections.
We believe books are not just for reading—they are for experiencing, questioning, and growing from.
