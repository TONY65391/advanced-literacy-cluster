About Us

More Than Just Reading

OUR CORE MISSION

The Advanced Literacy Cluster is a specialized human-capital development initiative. Our mission is to cultivate Autonomous Reading — the ability of a child to decode, comprehend, and solve problems within a text without external aid. We don't just teach children to read, we equip them with the resillence to understand a book independently, identifying learning gaps before they become academic hurdles.

How it Works

The Strategic Operations

"READING CLUSTERS" stands to ensure consistent cognitive growth, the program operates on a rigorous Monday - Thursday scehdule. Each age bracket is assigned a specific "Professional Sitting" window to build stamina and discipline.

Milestone Celebrations

By maintaining this schedule, students reach the target of 198 hours of self-study per term, a milestone celebrated at our high-profile Corporate Milestone Events.

Age Group The Objective Scheduled Sitting Window Duration
Ages 5-8 Foundational Fixing 7:00PM - 8:00PM 1HR
Ages 9-12 Cognitive Strengthening 7:00PM - 9:00PM 2HRS
Ages 13 - 17 Advanced Mastering 7:00PM - 10:00PM 3HRS
Ages 18 and above Pages Leafing 7:00PM - 11:00PM and above 4-5HRS and above
